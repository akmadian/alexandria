Why?
- Developing Alexandria isn't cheap, in time or in money (Claude Max 5x isn't cheap, and probably need to pay a subscription or fees just for the payment service itself lol, also need to keep the web domains)
- I'm building something of value, at least to myself, and hopefully to other people, I suppose it's not *unethical* to offer the opportunity for people to support me

How?
- "Pay what you want" model, I think. Lets everyone have the binaries, makes it accessible to everyone - a small creator or artist strapped for cash, all the way up to professional photographers and creators making good money.
- Offer crypto payments?

Payment processors
- Lemon Squeezy probably best

Ethical Tenets
- No DRM, no "phone home" license checking. No paywall gated features, no subsidization via training AI models with your data.
- EVERYONE gets automatic updates.
- Anonymous payments - no user accounts, no tracking. The only privacy vuln surface for payers is the payment service itself.
- Show the math behind the costs.
- Never lock anybody out. It's pay what you want, forever. We will never paywall features. We will never stop you from getting updates if you don't pay. And the beauty of open source is that if I start being an asshole for some reason, anyone can fork the repo and just roll their own version of it.

What do users get for paying?
- Gratitude :) 
- Name on a "supporters" list in the code, in the app, etc? Name or badge on the website?
- A solid, performant, well developed and actively maintained product that respects them and their data, and offers a best in class feature set.

UX
- Type zero friction: Suggested price is default, with explicit guidance that it's pay what you want, and that you CAN choose to pay zero. See elementary OS strategy.
- Transparency in costs: Tell users what their payment covers, not just "support me", but what exactly the payment goes to.
- Payment framing: it's a vote to support a more ethical, more open, more respectful computing future, controlled by the open source devs and their users, not corporations. Phrase it as like "pay for Alexandria with the cost of a lunch out, not with your privacy."
- Optionally attach your name to the payment, if you want to be added to the thanks lists
- Download link pops up after payment flow complete
- See elementary OS's model https://elementary.io/
