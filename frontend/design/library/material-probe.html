v2 after the neumorphism veto: NO gradients, NO inner highlights, NO control shadows. Dimension = pole anchoring (surfaces at the pole, ink near the other pole, fills 2–8% off) + two-grade lines used sparingly + fills-as-objects (fill XOR border, rounded) + steeper ink ladder.
